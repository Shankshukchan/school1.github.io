CODING

EMPOWERING YOUNG MINDS FOR A DIGITAL FUTURE

In the digital age, coding is an essential
skill that fosters creativity, problem solving, and analytical thinking. Following the NEP 2020, we at CWS Agra have partnered with Google CS First to offer a coding program from Early Years to Class Seven. This initiative empowers students with the tools they
need to excel in a technology-driven world, promoting curiosity, innovation, and critical thinking

CAREER COUNSELING

EMPOWERING STUDENTS THROUGH EXPERT COUNSELLING

Our Career and Guidance Counselling Program supports students at every stage. In Classes 9 and 10, students explore career options through workshops. In Class 11, boot camps help refine their profiles and degree choices. In Class 12, we assist with university applications, both local and international, ensuring they are well prepared for the future.

At CWS Agra, Physical Education is more than just exercise—it’s about discovering potential and fostering growth. With engaging activities and annual tournaments, students develop skills, confidence, and teamwork, building a strong foundation for a healthy and active lifestyle.

STUDENT COUNCIL

INSPIRING LEADERSHIP

At CWS Agra, leadership and responsibility are core values instilled in students through the Student Council. This dedicated group contributes actively to school events and decisions, fostering a sense of purpose and collaboration. The investiture ceremony marks the beginning of each academic year, celebrating the appointment of new Council members and their commitment to serving the school community

CLUBS 

The following clubs meet once a week in school to help children to learn and acquire life skills. The students of classes VI to X must choose to be a member of any one of the clubs as per their choice. The clubs offered are:

ENVIRONMENTAL CONSERVATION CLUB

ENVIRONMENTAL CONSERVATION CLUB

Promotes sustainability through recycling drives, clean-ups, tree plantations, and awareness campaigns to reduce the school’s environmental impact.

SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY CLUB

SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY CLUB

A space for exploring science through hands-on projects like robotics, coding, and model-making. Members develop innovation, problem solving skills, and scientific curiosity.

MUSIC & PERFORMANCE CLUB

MUSIC & PERFORMANCE CLUB

A platform for students to express their love for music and dance through singing, playing instruments, and performing, fostering creativity and confidence.

EXPRESSION CLUB

EXPRESSION CLUB

Focused on public speaking and drama, this club helps students build confidence, enhance communication, and develop teamwork through performances and creative activities.

ART & CRAFT CLUB

ART & CRAFT CLUB

A creative space for students to explore various art forms like painting, drawing, and DIY crafts, encouraging self expression and imagination.

SPORTS CLUB

SPORTS CLUB

Offers indoor activities like Badminton, Skating, Taekwondo, Table Tennis, Lawn Tennis, and Chess, plus outdoor games like Football, Swimming, Basketball, Volleyball, Cricket, and Boxing.

MATHLETIC CLUB

MATHLETIC CLUB

A hub for students passionate about math, where they solve puzzles, engage in problem solving sessions, and participate in competitions to boost critical thinking and teamwork.

PHILATELY CLUB

PHILATELY CLUB

A club for stamp enthusiasts to learn the cultural and historical significance of stamps, exchange collections, and participate in philatelic events.

HOUSE SYSTEM

At CWS Agra, students belong to one of four Houses throughout their school years, fostering community spirit, leadership, and holistic development.

The House system inspires a spirit of healthy competition through a variety of tournaments, events, and year-round challenges, with Houses striving to earn points. It is a cherished tradition in school life, promoting a sense of unity and often placing siblings in the same House to strengthen shared experiences and connections.

At Crimson World School, Agra, our inclusive admissions process welcomes students into a community focused on growth, innovation, and holistic excellence.
