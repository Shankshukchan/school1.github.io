ABOUT CWS AGRA

Crimson World School, Agra, stands as a beacon of educational excellence, blending tradition with innovation. As part of the renowned Crimson Schools network, our campus fosters a culture of holistic learning and personal growth. CWS Agra is dedicated to empowering students to become confident, skilled, and future-ready leaders.

We believe in creating a vibrant community where students, teachers, and staff collaborate with mutual respect and shared values. Our focus extends beyond academics and extra curriculars, ensuring that every learner thrives in an environment filled with opportunities for creativity, exploration, and growth.

Our goal is to provide enriching learning experiences that build a strong academic foundation and inspire lifelong success. At CWS Agra, we cultivate a safe, joyful, and inclusive space where every student feels valued, every success is celebrated, and individual needs are recognized and supported.

Our Vision

To believe in innovative learning for an evolving world, combining holistic education, traditional wisdom, and state-of-the-art technology to prepare students for a successful future.

VALUE TATEMENT

At CWS Agra, we believe in

  • To blend traditional wisdom with modern technology for comprehensive development
  • To prepare students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to succeed in their future endeavors.
  • To integrate the ideals of holistic education into our schooling system.

A MESSAGE FROM THE PRINCIPAL’S DESK


“The mind is not a vessel to be filled, but a fire to be kindled.” – Plutarch In every child, there is a world waiting to be explored. We are committed in our mission to inculcate a state of happiness in every child to ensure a peace-loving and harmonious world. We are devoted to set benchmarks in nurturing children to grow into strong, matured, kind and intelligent adults, who are original in their ideas and decisive in their actions.

We focus primarily on preparing our students to be worthy citizens, refined in heart and mind and skilled enough to achieve paramount goals. From the first tread into Grade Nursery at Crimson till the intermediate studies is indeed a preparation for a child to combat the challenges that the gigantic real world would pose.

We at Crimson aim to nurture our students so that they are well equipped to face the competitive society with value-based knowledge and skills that have contributed towards the growth and vision in building global citizens and a conscientious humanity.

As we venture ahead and spread our wings in this spectacular valley of value-based education our endeavour is to strike a balance between state of-the-art- infrastructure and a robust curriculum that opens the door to global platforms for our peace loving and problem-solving students. Activities and events related to value education, environment, health education, games and sports, visual and performing arts, culture and heritage, personality development are effectively woven into the main fabric of school curriculum.

As Principal, I would like to personally welcome you to our Crimson family. We hope that your child’s journey with us will surely help in yielding fruitful results and envisage our shared dreams and to dream anew.

MS. RITU TANDON
PRINCIPAL, CWS, AGRA
